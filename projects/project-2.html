Fitness Tracker MVP

From concept to App Store launch: Building a habit-forming fitness app for casual runners.

Role: Solo PM/Founder Timeline: 4 Months Team: 2 Freelance Devs

The Opportunity

Most fitness apps are too complex for beginners. Market research showed a gap for a simple, "no-frills" tracker that focuses purely on consistency rather than advanced performance metrics.

The MVP Strategy

We defined the MVP with a strict focus on core value: "Track a run in 2 taps."

  • Included: GPS tracking, time/distance, history log.
  • Excluded: Social sharing, coaching, premium plans (saved for V2).

Execution

Managed the backlog using Linear, running 1-week sprints. I handled QA and user testing personally on weekends. We used React Native to deploy to both iOS and Android simultaneously.

Outcome

Launched in January to capture New Year's revolutions.

  • 10,000+ downloads in Month 1 (Organic).
  • 25% Day-30 retention rate (higher than potential category benchmark of 15%).
  • Featured on "New Apps We Love" niche blog posts.
