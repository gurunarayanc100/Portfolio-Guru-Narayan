AI/ML Infrastructure Platform Strategy

Perfalign: Building a Hardware-Aware AI Performance Platform from 0 → Adoption

Establishing a unified, extensible platform for AI model performance analysis and deployment.

Overview

Perfalign is a modular AI model performance analysis and inference deployment platform built at MulticoreWare. I led Perfalign from initial opportunity discovery through product strategy, execution, and customer adoption—establishing it as a reusable platform embedded within semiconductor SDKs and used by OEMs and AI teams to benchmark and optimize workloads across hardware architectures.

Context

Organization Model

MulticoreWare operated in a matrix structure: Technology Units for engineering and Business Units for customer/BD. No dedicated product ops or marketing meant Perfalign had to be built end-to-end by aligning teams without direct authority.

The Mandate

As the first Product Manager, my goal was to establish product thinking and build reusable products for both customers and internal engineering teams.

Problem Discovery

I began with extensive discovery involving interviews and surveys with ~300 internal developers and ~100 external partners. This surfaced three systemic problems:

Visualization Gap

Model visualization tools did not scale to modern LLMs and transformer architectures.

Fragmented Tools

Hardware-specific tools were fragmented, inconsistent, or vendor-locked.

Manual Workflows

Optimization and profiling were manual, spread across multiple tools, and hard to reason about end-to-end.

Strategy & Key Decisions

The opportunity was not another point tool—but a unified, extensible platform that could adapt as models and hardware evolved.

Modular Architecture

Intentionally separated core orchestration, hardware-specific extensions, and analysis workflows.

Build-vs-Adopt

Integrated Google’s Model Explorer (Apache-licensed) rather than duplicating work, focusing investment on durable differentiation.

Execution & Operating Model

Clarity & Ownership

Established a customized product lifecycle and RASCI model. Authored clear PRDs for each module.

Multi-Layered Roadmaps

Tailored roadmaps for executives (strategy), GTM teams (sales), and engineering (modules).

Execution Cadence

  • Daily scrums and sprint rituals with developers
  • Weekly automated status updates to executives
  • Monthly exec-level product updates

Key Learnings

  • Matrix Alignment: Influencing without authority requires hyper-transparency and structured rituals.
  • Strategic Adoption: Leveraging open-source for non-core features accelerates GTM.
  • Persona Diversity: A platform must serve semiconductor vendors, OEMs, and developers equally.
